Overwatch

Intro

Overwatch is an animated FPS (First Person Shooter) that takes place in the future around the year 2090. It features a "cast" or pool of 25 different charecters (or heroes) that all have unique abilities that help change the course of a game. Each charecter has a unique backstory that is explained throughout voice interactions in the game, official comics released by the creators, posts made by the creators or official animated shorts that span about 8-10 minutes and are a sort of mini movie. I personally love the game and have been playing since a month after it's launch in June 2016. One of the main reasons I still play is because the game has at least one major update every month. These updates can very between a new map, a new charecter or drastically changing a charecters abilities if they are too strong.
